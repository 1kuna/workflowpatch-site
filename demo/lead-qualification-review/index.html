A small mock workflow that turns messy sales and marketing lead rows into a lead review ledger, approval queue, blocked leads, and error log. It uses no client data, no live outreach, no CRM writes, and no connected accounts.

Input

Six mock leads from forms, referrals, community replies, partner sheets, and a blocked scraped-list row.

Accepted

Two rows land in an approval queue for human review before any CRM update or outreach.

Blocked

Duplicate, unpermitted, scraped, and malformed rows are held before they can trigger action.
