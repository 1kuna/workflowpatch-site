Campaign feedback loop demo output.

A mock marketing workflow that turns campaign briefs, approved asset primitives, performance metrics, and AI draft candidates into a review-gated next-test ledger. No client data, no ESP credentials, no CRM writes, and no live social or email send.

Feedback Ledger CSV Review Queue CSV Blocked Output CSV Error Log CSV Brief Draft Runbook Source Script

Input

Mock campaign briefs, approved claims, banned terms, performance windows, and AI-style candidate next-test copy.

Output

Three candidate tests enter a feedback ledger with click rate, conversion rate, ROI after cost, decision, and row-level evidence.

Failure Path

Paused campaigns, stale data, and banned claims are blocked or routed to review before any customer-facing output.
